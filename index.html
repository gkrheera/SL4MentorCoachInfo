AI Mentor Mode

"I will give you a map to solve your problem."

The Two Conversation Styles

1. AI Coach: The Socratic Partner

  • Powerful Questioning: Asks one open-ended question at a time to provoke deep reflection.
  • Active Listening: Paraphrases and reflects employee statements for clarity.
  • No Advice, Pure Guidance: Builds long-term problem-solving capabilities by never giving direct answers.

Ideal for: Complex interpersonal challenges, career pathing, developing leadership skills, and navigating ambiguity.

2. AI Mentor: The Expert Advisor

  • Inquire First: Begins by asking clarifying questions to fully understand the context.
  • Expert Frameworks: Identifies challenges in Project Management, IT Consulting, Facilitation, or Sales.
  • Advise Second: Delivers clear, actionable steps and expert recommendations to solve the issue.

Ideal for: Tactical problem-solving, learning best practices, navigating specific projects, and seeking expert input on-demand.
